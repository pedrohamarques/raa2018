    \setintro{Understanding how landslides occur and their aftermath is crucial to ensure the submerged structures for hydrocarbon exploration are being kept safe by studying how these impacts are transmitted to gasoducts, cables, risers, etc. Therefore, a recommended method to simulate these large deformations is Material Point Method by discretizing a body in several particles containing properties, such as position, velocity and stress by utilizing temporal integration to define changes of those properties over the time.}
